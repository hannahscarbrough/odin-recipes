Chicken Alfredo Stuffed Shells

Description

Shredded, grilled chicken mixed in with alfredo sauce, ricotta cheese, and three other blends of cheeses. Chopped green onions, diced onions, and seasonings. Mixture is placed into jumbo pasta shells.

Ingredients

  • Package of Jumbo Shells
  • 4 cans of Classico alfredo sauce
  • Green onion
  • White onion
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Bag of mozarella
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pound of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • Parsley

*season to taste

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil water
  2. While water boils, rinse and pat dry chicken
  3. Get olive oil and drizzle a lil' on each thigh, front and back
  4. Season as you wish
  5. pan fry the chicken until cooked thoroughly
  6. In a big mixing bowl, mix together one can of alfredo sauce, cheese blend, half of ricotta cheese, and same seasonings used for chicken
  7. Get cutting board and dice white onion and green onion
  8. Put whole diced onion into mixture and only half of green onion dices. Save other half for later.
  9. Shred cooked chicken
  10. Throw shredded chicken into mixture
  11. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  12. Get a baking dish and pour a can of alfredo sauce on the bottom, just enough to cover it completely
  13. Time to start assembling shells! Grab a cooked shell and carefully spread thin layer of ricotta into shell
  14. Get a good spoonful of mixture and stuff the shell
  15. Repeat process until you run out of mixture
  16. Get another can of alfredo sauce and pour onto the shells, as much as you want
  17. Top it off with mozarella cheese and seasoning
  18. Bake at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes, or until cheese is golden brown
  19. Let it stand for 5 minutes once finished and then enjoy!!
