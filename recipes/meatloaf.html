Meatloaf

Description

Ground beef, mixed with seasonings, baked into the shape of a loaf.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds of Ground Beef
  • One Meatloaf Seasoning Packet
  • One Diced Yellow Onion
  • Half of a Red and Yellow Bell Pepper, diced thinly
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Breadcumbs
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • One Block of Pepperjack Cheese
  • One Block of Chedder Cheese
  • Two Eggs
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Dice up both blocks of cheeses into little cubes
  3. Get a large mixing bowl and throw in meatloaf packet, diced onion, diced bell peppers, diced up cheeses, two raw eggs, a cup of breadcrumbs, black pepper, garlic powder, tomato paste, and worcestershire sauce
  4. Knead the mixture together, until your heart's conent (my wife's words)
  5. Move mixture into a loaf container
  6. Spread a bit of ketchup on top and cover with foil
  7. Put in oven for 45-50 minutes
  8. At the 30 minute mark, remove the foil and place back in oven
  9. Once it's finished baking, take it out and let it sit for about 5 minutes
  10. Cut up a slice and enjoy!
